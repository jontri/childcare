General guidance for each coverage type listed below as follows (specific coverage may vary by child care center):

Full day: Minimum 6 hours continuous coverage 5 days/week (Mon - Fri). May depend on start and end times of coverage.

Half day: Minimum 4 hours continuous coverage 5 days/week (Mon - Fri). May depend on start and end times of coverage.

Infant: Coverage for children up to 1 1/2 years of age.

Night: Coverage between 7 pm and 5 am (following day).

Part time: Coverage less than 5 days/week (Mon - Fri).

School year only: Coverage provided only during the local school year. Coverage may not be available on days when local schools are closed.

Weekend: Coverage on Saturday, Sunday or both.
