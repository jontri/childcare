Karun Tahilramani

Founder and CEO

 

A father to two children, Karun is an immigrant of Indian descent. He is a strong proponent of high education standards during every phase of a child’s development and a staunch believer in equal opportunity for minorities and women in every segment of society. With a background in engineering, finance and management, he gave up a lucrative and influential career to support his wife in her pursuit of advanced training in medicine. It is during this sabbatical that he dealt with the challenges of finding high quality child care for his children, and eventually started Ratingsville.

Karun has thoroughly enjoyed staying home with his children since their birth and has taken a very active role in their upbringing. In fact, his vision for Ratingsville is driven by the desire to provide parents more time and opportunity, amid demanding work schedules, to stay actively engaged in the development and education of their children.

Karun holds a bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from Columbia University and a master's degree in Business Administration from the Darden School of Business (University of Virginia). He is an avid cook and wine enthusiast and currently resides in New York.
